# HOME

## What we are?

We are a small agency composed by three well experienced programmers joining efforts to deliver the best code quality in the market. We focus on deliver reliable, performant, and highly scalable code, whether it is refactoring or designing it from scratch.

## What do we do?

We focus in the design and development of low-level software including operating systems, libraries and tools. Whether it is for maintenance or design, we manage the full cycle of the code, from the design of the interfaces, implementation, testing to CI/CD and deployment.

Getting into specifics, we have a vast experiences in: Linux (POSIX) systems; RTOS/bare-metal embedded systems; CLI, TUI, and GUI tools; build environments; client-server applications and web backend among others.

The languages we manage are: C/C++, Rust, Go, Bash, Zig, Python, JS. Plus some other "minor" languages mainly oriented for internal use like: Lisp, Clojure, Nushell and others. Besides that we have expertise in many tools and frameworks of the industry, among them: Git, Docker, Makefile, SQL, Robot Framework, Nuttx, and more.
